IT & Cyber Security

A broad category involving any and all service types falling under the umbrella of cybersecurity. In IT, it’s the act of protecting networks and data from malicious attacks by deploying a variety of methods, from network monitoring, firewalls, anti-virus, layered networks, and more. Often managed through a provider, it can also be handled on-site.

Anti-Virus Protection

A subset of cybersecurity, anti-virus services are perhaps one of the most common types of IT service found in the industry. Anti-malware protection comes in the form of software and monitoring. Because of the evolving threat malware presents in the modern world, any company relying on IT must have some form of anti-virus installed or service used.
